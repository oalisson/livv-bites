# LIVV BITES — Brand Guide

> **Tagline:** Comfort food, thoughtfully made.
> **Produto:** Premium frozen Brazilian cheese bread (pão de queijo)
> **Mercado:** Estados Unidos — consumidor wellness-conscious

---

## Brand Essence

LIVV BITES was created to bring people back to what matters most:
real ingredients, meaningful moments, and food made with intention.

Inspired by the Brazilian tradition of pão de queijo and reimagined for today's wellness-conscious consumer, LIVV BITES combines comfort, quality, and simplicity in every bite.

---

## Why We Exist

We believe food should nourish more than hunger.

In a world filled with ultra-processed foods and endless ingredient lists, we wanted to create something different:

A comfort food made with ingredients you can recognize.

Something that feels nostalgic, yet aligns with modern values of wellness, quality, and mindful living.

LIVV BITES is our way of bringing people together around real food, one bite at a time.

---

## Mission

To elevate traditional Brazilian cheese bread through clean ingredients, thoughtful sourcing, and small-batch craftsmanship.

---

## Vision

To become the most trusted premium Brazilian cheese bread brand in the United States.

A brand known not only for exceptional taste, but for creating meaningful moments around the table.

## Brand Positioning

LIVV BITES is a premium, woman-owned frozen Brazilian cheese bread brand made with clean ingredients — bone broth, grass-fed cheeses, and organic eggs — and inspired by the simplicity of real food.

Unlike conventional frozen snacks, LIVV BITES combines authentic Brazilian tradition with a modern wellness mindset.

## Product Facts

> Oficial — extraído do packaging fornecido pelo fundador (2026-06-19).

- **Descritor:** LIVV BITES **GOURMET**

- **Ingredientes:** tapioca starch, grass-fed cheddar cheese, grass-fed parmesan cheese, organic bone broth, organic olive oil, organic eggs, salt
- **Contém:** leite, ovos
- **Selos:** Gluten Free · Clean Ingredients · No Artificial Preservatives · Made in Small Batches · Woman-Owned
- **Specs (SKU exemplo):** 12 peças · Net Wt 13 oz (370 g) · Keep Frozen
- **Nutrition (por serving de 2 peças / 50 g):** 159 cal · 9 g gordura · 16 g carbo · 5 g proteína
- **Preparo:** forno 350°F por 25–30 min · air fryer 350°F por 20–25 min (do congelado)
