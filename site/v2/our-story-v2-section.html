Our Story

Inspired by tradition.
Made for today.

LIVV BITES was born from my love for Brazilian cheese bread and a desire to share a better version with real ingredients and convenience. Everything we make is gluten-free, crafted in small batches, and made to bring comfort to your table.

What began in a small kitchen became a promise: honor the tradition we grew up with, while making it fit the way we eat today — clean, simple, and free of anything you can't recognize.

Every batch is still made with the same care as the first — because to us, this was never just about cheese bread. It's about the moments it creates around the table.
