So simple

From freezer to table,
in minutes.

No thawing, no fuss. Keep a bag in the freezer and you're three easy steps away from warm, golden Brazilian cheese bread.

  1. 1

    Keep it frozen

    Store in the freezer and bake straight from frozen — no thawing needed.

    Keep at 0°F (−18°C)
  2. 2

    Bake or air fry

    Pop them in the oven or air fryer until puffed and golden brown.

    350°F · 20–30 min
  3. 3

    Enjoy warm

    Crisp outside, soft and pillowy inside — perfect with coffee, any time of day.

    Best shared
Oven· 370°F · 25–30 min Air fryer· 350°F · 20–25 min
