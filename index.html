Our Story

It started at a
Brazilian table.

"The best food doesn't need a complicated list of ingredients — just real ones, and people to share it with."

Growing up in Brazil, pão de queijo meant warmth — slow mornings, family gathered close, something made with care. When we moved to the States, that comfort was hard to find without a label full of words we couldn't pronounce.

So we made it ourselves: the same golden, naturally gluten-free cheese bread, with real, recognizable ingredients — grass-fed cheese, organic eggs, and olive oil. Small-batch, woman-owned, and made the way we remember it.

What began in a small kitchen became a promise: to honor the tradition we grew up with, while making it fit the way we eat today — clean, simple, and free of anything we couldn't recognize.

Every batch is still made in small quantities, with the same care as the first. Because to us, this was never just about cheese bread — it's about the moments it creates around the table.
