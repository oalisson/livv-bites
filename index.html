Our Story

Inspired by tradition.
Made for today.

LIVV BITES was born from my love for Brazilian cheese bread and a desire to share a better version of this beloved tradition.

As a mother, I believe good food should be simple, nourishing, and made with ingredients you can trust. That’s why every batch is crafted with care, using real ingredients and a commitment to quality.

Made in small batches in Miami, LIVV BITES brings the comfort of Brazilian tradition to modern everyday moments — one bite at a time.
